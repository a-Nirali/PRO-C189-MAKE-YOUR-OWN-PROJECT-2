Traditional Clothing In India

India is a land of culture and heritage. Diverse communities live in India. As correctly said, India is the perfect example of a land with ‘unity in diversity.’ Various forms of traditions followed customs believed, food made, languages spoken, and clothing worn. Indians are known for their rich traditional beliefs and attires. Traditional attires may include the extravagant Sabyasachi lehenga or a simple khadi Gramin dhoti. Indian wardrobes also vary from one state to another, from one region to another. Every place has its unique clothes and style.
